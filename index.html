About Me

Regina grew up and studied in Auckland, New Zealand graduating from the University of Auckland with a Bachelor in Town Planning. I moved to Los Angeles two years ago to study CAD design after spending time in Bangkok studying Jewelry Design and Gemology.

I currently work as a designer and book keeper in the diamond and jewelry wholesale business. I have found that as much as I like to design, it is more of a hobby and looking for a career change.

In my spare time, you'll find me on hikes or dog parks with my two husky pups. I'm also a foodie who loves brunch so you can also find me drinking mimosas at a cafe most weekends. If I'm doing either of those things, then I'm exploring California with a camera in hand.
